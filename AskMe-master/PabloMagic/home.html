  1. About

AskMe is designed to be something like a hybrid of Facebook and WebMD. A social network that connects doctors and random strangers, to help expand the public’s knowledge on their ailments from the most reliable sources we know. The extended impact also includes highlighting accomplished persons with exemplary medical knowledge and, most importantly, reduce superfluous doctor’s visits that would save physicians’ time and patients’ money.
